Control Instructions

  • ✊ Make fist for Play/Pause
  • ✌️ Thumb up / Victory for Volume Up
  • 👎 Thumb down for Volume Down
  • 🎤 Say "Play" or "Pause" to control playback
  • 🎤 Say "Volume up" or "Increase volume"
  • 🎤 Say "Volume down" or "Decrease volume"
  • 🎤 Say "Skip forward" or "Forward"
  • 🎤 Say "Skip backward" or "Back"
